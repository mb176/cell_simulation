

-The area always has the origin in the bottom left corner, i.e. extends into the top right quadrant


-All length are multiples of the cell diameter, i.e. the area covered by a cell is 0.785398.

-Parameter files have to be of the form :
paramName1          : paramValue1
...

It cannot contain empty lines, and there has to be exactly one white space between : and the value. The maximum line length is 200 characters, and each variable can have at most 50 characters. The ordering matters and has to match the one used by SetParameters().
